# Dev-Style Play Store Asset Specifications

## Overview
This specification defines the "Developer/Prototype" style for the remaining Play Store assets: Feature Graphic and Screenshots. The goal is to provide valid, high-resolution assets that meet Play Console requirements while clearly indicating the application is in a pre-release or development state.

## Aesthetic ("The Blueprinted Wireframe")
*   **Background:** Dark Blueprint Blue (`#001133`) or Terminal Black (`#101010`).
*   **Grid:** Fine light blue or grey grid lines every 64px.
*   **UI Elements:** High-contrast wireframes (white outlines). No fill or semi-transparent fills.
*   **Typography:** Monospace (Consolas/Courier).
*   **Debug Overlays:** Visible "resolution" watermarks (e.g., "1080x1920") and "safe area" guides.

## Asset Requirements

### 1. Feature Graphic
*   **Dimensions:** 1024 x 500 pixels.
*   **Content:** Large app title centered. "DEV BUILD" stamp.
*   **Style:** Dark background, subtle grid, "tech" corners.

### 2. Phone Screenshots (Portrait)
*   **Dimensions:** 1080 x 1920 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio).
*   **Quantity:** 2 distinct screens.
    *   *Screen 1 (Home):* Mockup of the "My Repositories" list.
    *   *Screen 2 (Details):* Mockup of the "Repo Details" view.
*   **Style:** Wireframe representation of the UI components (TopAppBar, Cards, Floating Action Buttons).

### 3. Tablet 7-inch Screenshots (Landscape)
*   **Dimensions:** 1280 x 800 pixels.
*   **Quantity:** 1 screen.
*   **Content:** Adapted "Home" layout for wider screens (e.g., Grid view hint).

### 4. Tablet 10-inch Screenshots (Landscape)
*   **Dimensions:** 1920 x 1200 pixels.
*   **Quantity:** 1 screen.
*   **Content:** "Dashboard" style wireframe layout.

## Technical Details
*   **Format:** PNG (24-bit, no alpha).
*   **Color Profile:** sRGB.
