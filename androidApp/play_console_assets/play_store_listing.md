# Google Play Store Listing

## Title
QA Verify and Track

## Short Description
Track your GitHub repository builds and QA status on the go.

## Full Description
QA Verify and Track is the ultimate companion for developers and QA engineers. Connect your GitHub repositories to monitor build statuses, track deployed applications, and manage your QA workflows directly from your Android device.

**Key Features:**
*   **GitHub Integration:** Securely connect using your Personal Access Token (PAT).
*   **Repository Management:** View and manage multiple repositories in one place.
*   **Build Tracking:** Keep track of your latest app builds, versions, and platforms (Android/iOS).
*   **Custom Configuration:** Set global defaults or configure tokens per repository for granular access control.
*   **Secure:** Your tokens are stored securely on your device.

Stay on top of your development cycle with QA Verify and Track. Download now and streamline your QA process!
